# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
AgentCapTune community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment: demonstrating
empathy and kindness, respecting differing opinions, giving and gracefully
accepting constructive feedback, and focusing on what is best for the community.
Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting/derogatory comments,
and publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported
to the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and
fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of reporters.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
